About ScanMe

ScanMe is a lightweight, secure QR code scanner and generator PWA (Progressive Web Application) designed for everyone.

Features:

  • Real-time QR code scanning with camera
  • Generate custom QR codes from text or URLs
  • Auto-redirect to scanned URLs
  • Download QR codes as PNG images
  • Full offline support via PWA
  • Secure authentication
  • History tracking
